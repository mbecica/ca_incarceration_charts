California Community Corrections Population, 2011–2024

Adults under non-incarcerated supervision: parole, probation, PRCS, mandatory supervision, and CRPP

Sources: CDCR Monthly Report of Population & Data Points; LAO; CA DOJ OpenJustice (probation); CPOC California Probation Summary; LAO Figure 1 (PRCS).
AB 109 (Public Safety Realignment) created PRCS and Mandatory Supervision in October 2011; CRPP began mid-2016. PRCS and MS shown from 2013 (first year with verified CPOC counts).
All values are December snapshots except PRCS/MS (June 30 from CPOC; quarterly average from LAO 2019–2023).
