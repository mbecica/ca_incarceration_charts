California Incarceration Population, 1920–2025

State prison, state parole, county jail, and county probation populations

Sources: CDCR historical records (Zimring & Hawkins compilation); BJS Annual Parole Survey; CA DOJ OpenJustice; CA Board of State and Community Corrections (BSCC) Jail Profile Survey.
Parole data available from 2000; probation from 2003; county jail from 2000.
State Parole includes CRPP supervision (data available from 2016). County Probation includes PRCS and mandatory supervision, both created by AB 109 (Public Safety Realignment, 2011); data available from 2013.
