California Community Corrections Cost per Person, FY 2011–12 to 2023–24

Annual spending per supervised person, 2024 dollars

State parole = CDCR Parole Operations + Board of Parole Hearings (all funds) ÷ Dec parole population.
County supervision = sum of all 58 counties' probation expenditures (all funds) ÷ combined probation + PRCS + mandatory supervision population. The county budget is treated as funding all county-supervised populations together because PRCS and mandatory supervision do not have separate funding lines.
Sources: Governor's Budget chapter 5210; CA State Controller's Office (uctr-c2j8 + ju3w-4gxp); CDCR Monthly Report of Population; CA DOJ OpenJustice; CPOC; LAO. Adjusted to 2024 dollars using BLS CPI-U fiscal-year averages.
