California Justice-Involved Population by Type, 2003–2024

Share of total justice-involved population in state prison, state parole, county jail, and county probation

Sources: CDCR historical records; BJS Annual Parole Survey; CA DOJ OpenJustice; CA Board of State and Community Corrections (BSCC) Jail Profile Survey.
Total is the sum of state prison, state parole, county jail, and county probation populations.
State Parole includes CRPP supervision (data available from 2016). County Probation includes PRCS and mandatory supervision, both created by AB 109 (Public Safety Realignment, 2011); data available from 2013.
